Pelagios (Greek for 'of the Sea') is a community network that facilitates linking of online resources that document the past, based on the places they refer to. This Website is a demo search and exploration tool, running on a limited test set of classical literature, historic maps, and information from databases of historic places and artefacts from our partner network. Start by searching for a place, object or topic you are interested in. Narrow down your search, or combine multiple searches to explore similarities, differences and connections.
